It's recommended to backup your contacts before proceeding. For more information, please visit:
http://qontactsapp.com/

To start, tap "Analyze Contacts" button, to start analyzing your contacts. If any of your contacts needs to be updated, it'll be displayed next.

You can preview each contact's updated numbers by tapping the name.

To do a batch update of your contacts, you can tap the check mark to mark it, tap it again to unmark it. When you're done, tap "Update" button.
